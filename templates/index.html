Maryland Public High School Characteristics

Based on Data from 2021 - 2022

Welcome to the State of Maryland's high school viewer! Using this interactive tool, you can view information about high schools across the state.

(1) Zoom in on the interative map and hover over the markers to view information about each school.
(2) Use the drop down menu to select the high school locale to view summary information for that locale.
